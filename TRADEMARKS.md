not grant trademark rights.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the Odogwu HQ name only to:

- truthfully refer to the original upstream project;
- say that your project is forked from or compatible with Odogwu HQ;
- preserve attribution and required legal notices.

## Not Allowed

You may not:

- name a fork, service, app, package, domain, social account, or community in a
  way that suggests it is the official Odogwu HQ project;
- use Odogwu HQ logos, icons, screenshots, or brand assets in a fork;
- imply endorsement, partnership, sponsorship, or affiliation;
- use confusingly similar names, branding, domains, or package names;
- use Odogwu HQ branding for any commercial purpose.

## Fork Naming

Public forks should use a clearly different name and should state:

> This is an unofficial fork of Odogwu HQ and is not affiliated with Marvengrey
> Technologies Nig LTD.

The project owner may ask you to rename or rebrand any fork, package, domain, or
public presentation that creates confusion.
