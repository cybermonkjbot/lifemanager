# Use Policy

This policy summarizes how Odogwu HQ may and may not be used. The legal terms
are in [LICENSE](./LICENSE). If this summary conflicts with the license, the
license controls.

## Allowed Without Written Permission

You may:

- read and study the source code;
- fork the repository;
- modify the software;
- run and self-host the software for personal, noncommercial use;
- share noncommercial forks or patches so long as you preserve the license,
  copyright notices, and required notices;
- contribute changes back to the upstream project.

## Not Allowed Without Written Permission

You may not:

- use Odogwu HQ for commercial purposes;
- use Odogwu HQ inside a company or for business operations;
- sell, rent, lease, sublicense, or resell Odogwu HQ or any fork;
- provide Odogwu HQ as a hosted service, managed service, SaaS product,
  agency deliverable, client deployment, consulting deliverable, or white-label
  product;
- charge for installation, hosting, maintenance, training, support, access, or
  customization of Odogwu HQ;
- bundle Odogwu HQ with a paid product or service;
- use the source, documentation, assets, or generated behavior to train,
  fine-tune, benchmark, or improve a commercial AI or machine-learning product;
- remove license, copyright, attribution, security, or source-available notices;
- use Odogwu HQ names, logos, domains, branding, or trade dress except as
  allowed by [TRADEMARKS.md](./TRADEMARKS.md).

## Self-Hosting

Self-hosting is free when the use is noncommercial. This includes personal
servers, local machines, and personal cloud accounts.

Self-hosting is not allowed when it is for a business, employer, client,
customer, paid community, agency, consultancy, or other commercial activity.

## Questions

If you are unsure whether a use is commercial, assume it is not allowed unless
the project owner confirms otherwise in writing.
